Welcome to T- John Institute of Technology

  • Establishment Year : 2006
  • Approved by: AICTE
  • Affiliation :Visvesvaraya Technological University(VTU)
  • Location: #86/1, Kammnahalli,Gottigere, Bannerghatta Road, Bangalore, Karnataka 560 083
  • Website : http://www.tjohnit.com

T. John Institute of Technology is part of the T. John Group of Institutions. Since a humble beginning in 1993 the T. John Group has expanded to include new departments and blocks and spread over an area of 20 acres. TJIT was set up in the year 2006 in order to meet the rapidly growing need for IT Professionals in India and abroad. Starting with just two courses B.E. Computer Science Engineering and B.E. Electronics and Communication Engineering, today the college has expanded into a full fledged provider of technical education, offering B.E. degrees in six streams of engineering, as well as two post graduate courses.

Engineering Branches Available :

  • Computer Science & Engineering (C.S.E.)
  • Information Science & Engineering (I.S.E.)
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering (E.C.E)
  • Mechanical Engineering (M.E.)
  • Civil Engineering (CE)

Post Graduate Programs Available :

  • M.Tech in Machine Design.
  • M.Tech in Digital Communication & Network.
  • M.Tech in VLSI Design & Embedded Systems.
  • M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering.
  • M.Tech in Computer Network Engineering.

Placments :

Placement and Training department serves as a link between Engineering graduates and industry. We organize seminars, Technical Workshops, Personality Development Programs and other Training Programs.

Apart from the normal placement activities, we have a frequent personal interaction with the students which in turn help to understand & realize their problems better and try to solve effectively & boosting their confidence in every aspect.

We ensure that the students are trained according to the requirements of the industry before they appear for the campus placement.

A step by step training methodology is adopted to develop their aptitude skills, reasoning skills, communication skills, leadership qualities, interpersonal skills, technical skills and finally the interview tackling skills. Basic sessions on Career development is carried out along with their academic curriculum and a rigorous 1-2 week training on the above mentioned skill sets by the industry experts is organized when the students are in their seventh semester.
