Welcome to Sri Vidya Vinayaka Institute of Technology

  • Establishment Year : 2013
  • Approved by: AICTE

  • Location: #11,Vinayaka Nagar,Dodda Aaladamara Road Bangalore-560074
  • Website : http://www.srivvit.com/

The college is promoted by highly educated personalities.SVVIT is located in the silicon city,Bangalore and is just about 20 kms from the center of the city.

The college is situated within the sprawling five acres of garden, rich in greenery. The campus is ideal for academics, cultural and sports activities. The college building includes the spacious class rooms, laboratories, workshop, libraries, cafeteria and other facilities. The college is very well connected by the BMTC Bus facilities from all parts of Bangalore City
